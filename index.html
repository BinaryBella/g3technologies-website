Our History

Discover the milestones that have shaped our journey from a small startup to an industry leader

Milestones 1-5 of 10
2011
First Client, First Project
We were fortunate to receive our first paid project from the same client we worked with during our university project—our first step into the professional world.
2016
First Local Recognition
We were honored with the e-Swabhimani Gold Award in two categories—Entertainment & Lifestyle and Inclusion & Empowerment—recognizing our innovative contributions to digital content in Sri Lanka
2011
The Formation
As undergraduates, we were tasked with a real-world group project. Our team, designated as Group 3, inspired the name "G3 Team." This project marked the beginning of our journey together.
2014
The First Official Step
We established our partnership company in Malabe, laying the foundation for what would eventually become G3 Technologies.
2016
International Achievement
Our work gained global acclaim when we secured First Place in the Inclusion & Empowerment category at the prestigious mBillionth Awards, celebrating excellence in mobile innovations across South Asia.
2018–2019
Team Expansion & Global Clients
Our growing team enabled us to collaborate with major international clients, including China Harbour and the Port City development projects.
2023
Back in Action
With renewed energy and a revitalized team, we returned stronger and more focused, ready to rebuild and grow.
2017
Incorporation as G3 Technologies
Our partnership company transitioned into an incorporated entity—G3 Technologies (Pvt) Ltd—in June 2017, marking a significant leap in our business journey.
2020–2022
Navigating Crisis
The COVID-19 pandemic and Sri Lanka’s economic crisis hit hard. G3 Technologies faced a difficult period, operating in survival mode during nationwide lockdowns and economic instability.
2024 & Beyond
A New Chapter Begins
With fresh talent and ambitious goals, G3 Technologies is entering a new era—targeting bold achievements and long-term impact in the tech industry
